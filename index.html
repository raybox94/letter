To Isra,

What words can capture the depth of my feelings for you? It’s astonishing that, despite my life’s work revolving around the written word, I struggle to articulate the profound love and desire I hold for you. From the very moment I laid eyes on you, you became the sun that brightens my world radiant and beautiful.

I miss you in a way that is both simple and desperate, more than I ever thought possible. I thought I could prepare myself for this longing, but each day it grows deeper. This letter is a mere echo of my heart's pain, a testament to how integral you have become to my existence.

I know you may hear these words often, but I need you to understand the truth behind them: I love you. I love you more than words can express, more than I ever thought I could love someone.

So I ask you, be my love, today and always.

Forever yours,

Rayaan
